Reel 2004

Network Seven - Various

I worked at Network Seven from 2001 to 2004 in the 3D visual effects department. Part of a 3 Person team we were responsible for creating visual effects for:

  • Program titles and graphic packages
  • Station on air Idents
  • Station branding
  • On air promotion
  • Special Events TV Spots
  • Misc visual effects (visualizations, real time sets, Research and Development)

Duties:
As part of a small team we were required to be a generalist and be able to complete a project from start to finish; from planning and previs, matchmove, mattepaint, animation, FX, lookdev lighting right to compositing. On larger projects we would specialize where our strengths were, for me it was lighting and compositing.

Projects can range from 1 day to 3 months in length/budget. We were expected to work extremely efficiently and know where to cut corners when we can whilst still keeping a standard the station has came accustomed to.

Awards:
Torino Winter Olympics titles - Winner Gold PROMAX; Best Special Event Titles
Athens Olympics titles - Winner Gold AEAF; Best Program Titles 2004
Salt Lake Winter Olympics titles - Winner Gold AEAF; Best Program Titles 2002
Station Idents - Numerious Promax awards.
